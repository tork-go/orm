  Against SQLAlchemy, Prisma, Drizzle and EF Core, there are no structural gaps left in the query API. What remains is vocabulary and one bounded feature:

  - Table aliasing → self-joins, plus joining two tables with no declared relationship. The largest remaining item, but a bounded addition rather than new shape.
  - Window vocabulary — LAG, LEAD, NTILE, SUM() OVER, frame clauses
  - NULLS FIRST/NULLS LAST, RIGHT/FULL OUTER JOIN, DISTINCT ON
  - Having expressiveness — no OR, no aggregate-vs-aggregate
  - Recursive CTEs — still needs a way to name a virtual table's columns
  - Unchanged non-goals: change tracking, lazy loading, polymorphic mapping

  Self-joins would be the natural Phase 10 if you want to keep going.
